We've Rebranded

Formerly Silvah Studios. Now better than ever as RACINGSLOTH.

Having spent a lot of time and interest in our previous production studio, we have decided to rebrand and restart. With a little more experience under our belts, and a strong vision in ﻿front of us, we expect to hit the ground running.﻿

The reason behind our decision to restart is quite simple:

When we started to create films together, we wanted them to be different from other indie film production studios.

Enter RACINGSLOTH.

With Racing Sloth, we are going to take each production with the attention and care it deserves. Here's to RACINGSLOTH!
