What We Do

We are a brand new film production group. We make short films. We are also home to the new webseries The Studio. We have rebranded and restarted. We're back. Rested. Refueled. Ready to go.

Who We Are

A group of students with an ambition. We were formerly Silvah Studios. At that point, we had no aim. Although, we worked on our own volition. We had aspirations to make films, so we did. But, it had no order. They were unscripted and only appealed to a small audience. Rebranded as RACINGSLOTH, we look forward to making genuine films that appeal to a larger audience. With that much more maturity.
