Moving Away
from Silvah Studios

Having spent a lot of time and interest in our previous production studio, we have decided to rebrand and restart.
With a little more experience under our belts, and a strong vision in front of us, we expect to hit the ground running.
The reason behind our decision to restart is quite simple: When we started to create films together, we wanted them to be different from other indie film production studios.
Although our videos received positive reception and viewership, we felt the content did not appeal to the audience as well as we thought it would. Lacking plot, depth, and planning, Silvah Studios will be succeeded by RACINGSLOTH, a production company whose inception has provoked a positive reaction from the community.
