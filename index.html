welcome

Having spent a lot of time and interest in our previous production studio, we have decided to rebrand and restart. With a little more experience under our belts, and a strong vision in front of us, we expect to hit the ground running. The reason behind our decision to restart is quite simple: When we started to create films together, we wanted them to be different from other indie film production studios. Although our videos received positive reception and viewership, we felt the content lacked the plot, depth, and planning that we needed to reach our goals as a whole. Thus, Silvah Studios will be succeeded by Racing Sloth.

Rested, refueled, and ready to go. We are ready to put out some great new content. We're hype and we hope you are too!
