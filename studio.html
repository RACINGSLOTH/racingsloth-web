Our Maiden Voyage

The beginning of many more things to come.

We at Racing Sloth have created and been a part of countless sketch comedies, short films, and more. But we never quite understood the significance of a story.

But we certainly figured it out at some point because we are proud to announce that we are currently in the works of developing a brand new web-series: The Studio. For us as individuals, it certainly is our biggest project yet, but as Racing Sloth it's our first one. It's definitely going to take some time and whole lot of effort to make this a reality, but we're doing our best to make it happen. We will be holding auditions and special social media advertising campaigns, so stay tuned!
